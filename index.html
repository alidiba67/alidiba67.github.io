Ali Diba

I am post-doctoral research scientist at KU Leuven, where I work on computer vision and machine learning.

At KU Leuven, I worked with Prof. Luc Van Gool on different projects such as multi-task learning vision models on edge devices and also large sclae holistic video understanding. Previously I co-founded Sensifai, an computer vision startup, for large scale media archieve monitoring.
